Why confidential computing changes the game for legal AI

Every legal AI vendor promises security. Most mean TLS in transit and encryption at rest. That leaves a gap: the moment data is decrypted for processing, it is exposed to the operator, the cloud provider, and any compromised infrastructure.

Mizan eliminates that gap. AWS Nitro Enclaves create an isolated compute environment with no persistent storage, no operator SSH access, and no network connectivity beyond the attested channel. The enclave's integrity is verified cryptographically before any data enters.
