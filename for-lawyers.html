Why Confidentiality Matters

The gap every legal AI leaves open

Every legal AI platform promises security. They mean TLS in transit and encryption at rest. But AI cannot work with ciphertext. Before the model processes your query, the data must be decrypted. At that moment, it is exposed. To the vendor. To the cloud provider. To any attacker who has compromised the infrastructure. Mizan closes that gap entirely: the AI model runs locally on your machine. There is no server to compromise.

01
The Decryption Gap
AI models cannot process encrypted data. Every other platform decrypts your legal queries on a server they control, exposing your client matters to their infrastructure and anyone who compromises it. An opportunist hacker intercepting traffic at that layer sees everything.
The Problem
02
The Local AI Approach
Mizan runs the AI model directly on your machine. Your documents and queries never leave your device to be processed. There is no network hop, no cloud endpoint, nothing to intercept. The model processes everything locally and the result is rendered in your browser.

Data Protection

Lockdown Mode protects your screen when you step away

Attorney data doesn't stop being sensitive the moment you leave your desk. After 15 minutes of inactivity, Mizan automatically locks the entire interface, blanking all client matter content from view.

To resume, the attorney must verify their identity by sending a code to their registered email address, or sign in again from scratch. No timeout override. No PIN fallback.
